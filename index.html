Welcome to my site!

My name is Marieli Pereda. I studied fashion design at CENTRO University in Mexico City. At third semester I started working at Dracula Studio as a creative designer. When I was 21 I started my own purses brand and I'm currently working on some new projects. My journey has not been easy. I started studying business knowing that fashion and design was truly my passion. After almost two years I was lucky enough to pursue my dreams, and today I am finally fulfilling them. I am a positive, determined, tenacious, dedicated person who does everything with passion and energy.
